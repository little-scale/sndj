sndj spcexport — .sndj → WAV / .spc

Drop a .sndj song (or a .srm save and pick a slot) plus the matching sndj.sfc ROM (for its sample pool). WAV renders the song offline through the reference sequencer and the bit-exact S-DSP model. .spc captures the sequencer's DSP register stream for one song loop and bakes it — with the song's samples and a tiny SPC700 replayer — into a standard .spc that plays in any SPC player. Everything runs locally.
